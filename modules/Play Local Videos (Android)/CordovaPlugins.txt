This module will need these plugins:
////////////////////////////////////


com.dawsonloudon.videoplayer
https://github.com/dawsonloudon/VideoPlayer.git



For PhoneGap Build you can use:
///////////////////////////////


This plugin is only for Android, iOS can play local videos without any plugins.
<gap:plugin name="com.dawsonloudon.videoplayer"  />


INSTRUCTIONS
/////////////

Make sure:

- Your filenames doesn't contain spaces and special characters.

- That all filenames are unique and doesn't contain period (.) delimiters. Android considers video.lowres.mp4 and video.highres.mp4 the same file.

- Your videos have the mpeg4 codec.
